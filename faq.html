Frequently Asked Questions

What can I not donate?

Each charity has slightly different regulations around what items can or cannot be donated. Please refer to the individual charity websites.

General guide for what cannot be donated:

  • Ripped, torn, soiled or stained clothing
  • Broken, ripped or stained furniture
  • Cracked or broken homewares
  • Stained bedding including mattresses
  • Green waste
  • Household waste
  • Knives (except table cutlery)
  • Weapons (including replicas and martial arts weapons)
  • Taxidermy animals
  • Batteries

Do some charities collect?

Yes, some do. Please refer to the individual charity websites for collection information.

Can I donate in store?

Yes, please refer to the individual charity website of open times.

How do charities get listed on Gifta?

Registered charities can email Kinda Communities via hello@kindacommunities.com.au to enquire about listing on Gifta.

Listing on Gifta is free for all approved charities.
